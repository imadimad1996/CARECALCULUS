# Pricing — CareCalculus Clinical Decision Support

> CareCalculus provides open-access, evidence-based clinical calculators for ICU, ER, and hospital clinicians. High-value EHR documentation tools are available via CareCalculus PRO.

## Free Tier (Open Access)
- **Price:** $0 / Free
- **Target Audience:** Medical Students, Residents, Nurses, Physicians
- **Features Included:**
  - Unlimited access to all 100+ clinical decision support calculators, risk scores, and medical converters.
  - Standard ASCII and plain text report exports.
  - Full evidence citations (AHA, ESC, KDIGO, SFAR, BTS).
  - Offline-ready Progressive Web App (PWA) support.
  - Multilingual support in English, French, and Arabic.

## CareCalculus PRO (Clinical Efficiency Pass)
- **Pricing Options:**
  - **1-Month Pass:** $9.99 / month (non-renewing, one-time checkout)
  - **1-Year Pass:** $79.00 / year (save over 34%, one-time checkout)
- **Target Audience:** Attending Physicians, Hospitalists, ER & ICU Specialists, Busy Residents
- **Features Included:**
  - Everything in Free Tier.
  - **1-Click Epic / Cerner DotPhrase Exports (`.carecalculus` templates):** Copy pre-formatted clinical notes directly into EHR systems.
  - **Structured SBAR Handover Exports:** Copy formatted Situation-Background-Assessment-Recommendation notes for shift changes.
  - **Shift Queue Record Storage:** Save up to 50 patient scoring sessions locally per shift.
  - **Priority Feature Requests & Support.**
